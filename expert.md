*   **Embryonic Development:** Tracking "Clue Chain" development from Stage S0 (Laying) to S6 (Post-Pipping).
*   **TSD Management:** Precision monitoring for species where sex is determined by temperature (TSD). UI must warn if temp drifts outside the ±2°F "Pivot Zone."
*   **Hydration Dynamics:** Softshell species (*Apalone*) require precise moisture levels; excessive drying leads to early pipping failure.
*   **Hatching (Pipping) Protocol:**
    *   **External Pipp:** First crack in shell (Stage S5).
    *   **Assisted Hatching:** Only for endangered species (*Ornate Box*) when pipping exceeds 48 hours without progress.
