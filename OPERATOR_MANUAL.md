# 🐢 WINC Incubator Vault: Operator's Manual (v7.2.0)
Welcome to the Incubator Vault. This guide will help you use the app to track our turtle eggs from intake to hatching.

## 1. Getting Started (Clocking In)
1. **Launch the App**: Open the link on your tablet or computer.
2. **Identify Yourself**: Select your name from the dropdown list on the first screen. The app will remember who you are for the rest of your shift.

## 2. The Daily Protocol (The "Weight-First" Rule)
Every day, we check the health of our turtle bins.
1. **Go to Observations**: Click "Observations" in the sidebar.
2. **Weigh the Bin**: Before you can see the egg list, you must place the physical plastic bin on the scale.
3. **Log the Weight**: Enter the weight in the app. If the bin is "underweight" (dry), the app will tell you exactly how much water to add.
4. **Unlock**: Once the weight is logged, the egg grid will appear.

## 3. The Intake Wizard (New Turtles)
When a mother turtle arrives at WINC:
1. **Click "New Intake"**: This is a 2-step wizard.
2. **Source Info**: Enter the Species and the Case Number from the intake paperwork.
3. **Bin Setup**: Enter the "Target Weight" (the weight of the bin when it's perfectly moist).
4. **Egg Count**: Enter how many eggs you are putting in. The app will automatically label them (e.g., Egg 1, Egg 2).
5. **Finalize**: Hit "Commit to Vault." The eggs are now live.

## 4. The Daily Loop (Checking Eggs)
1. **Multi-Select**: You can click the checkboxes next to several eggs at once.
2. **The Action Tray**: A window will slide up at the bottom. 
3. **Update**: Change the "Stage" (e.g., S2 - Developing) or the "Status" for all selected eggs at once.
4. **Save**: Click "Apply to Batch" to save your work.

## 5. What if an Egg Hatches?
1. **Select the Egg**: Find the lucky egg in the observation grid.
2. **Change Status**: Set the status to **"Hatched."**
3. **Fill the Ledger**: A new screen will ask for the "Hatch Weight" and "Vitality." Once you save this, the egg is officially moved to the Hatchling Ledger for the rehab team.

## 6. Pro-Tips for Success
- **Look for the 🔒**: If you see a lock icon in Settings, it means we have active eggs. You cannot change biological lists (like species names) while eggs are in the incubator.
- **Tooltips**: Hover your mouse over any question mark (?) icon for a simple explanation of what that field means.
- **Accidents**: If you enter the wrong data, go to the **Settings > Audit** log to see what was recorded, then notify a Lead Biologist to fix the ledger.
